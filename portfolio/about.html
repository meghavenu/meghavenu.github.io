My Story

I'm a third-year B.Tech student in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science at Kumaraguru College of Technology, Coimbatore. Maintaining a CGPA of 8.61, I've been recognised with the Mahatma Gandhi Merit Scholarship for two consecutive years.

My passion is at the intersection of machine learning, natural language processing, and cloud AI. I don't just build models — I architect systems that perceive, reason, and act in the real world.

From a multimodal RAG chatbot that understands both text and diagrams, to a predictive retail analytics platform with real-time customer intelligence — I chase problems that force me to grow.

Outside code, I lead research initiatives, collaborate with peers, and look for any excuse to talk about the future of AI. I'm actively seeking internships and collaborative opportunities.
